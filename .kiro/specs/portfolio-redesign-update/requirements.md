# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This document outlines the requirements for updating Carlos R Gomez's online CV portfolio to reflect his recent professional growth, advanced studies in AI/ML, and leadership experience in security engineering. The updated portfolio will showcase his evolution from a security engineer to a security engineering manager with data-driven leadership capabilities, incorporating modern design principles inspired by leading portfolio examples.

## Glossary

- **Portfolio_System**: The complete online CV portfolio website including all pages, components, and interactive elements
- **Content_Management**: The system for organizing and presenting professional information including experience, education, projects, and skills
- **Design_Framework**: The visual and interactive design system that governs layout, typography, colors, and user experience
- **Professional_Profile**: The comprehensive representation of Carlos's career progression, skills, and achievements
- **Navigation_System**: The user interface elements that allow visitors to browse through different sections of the portfolio
- **Contact_Interface**: The system that enables potential employers or collaborators to reach out to Carlos
- **Project_Showcase**: The dedicated section displaying Carlos's key projects and achievements with detailed descriptions
- **Skills_Matrix**: The organized presentation of technical skills, leadership capabilities, and professional competencies

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a potential employer or collaborator, I want to quickly understand Carlos's current role and expertise, so that I can assess his fit for security engineering leadership positions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a visitor lands on the homepage, THE Portfolio_System SHALL display Carlos's current title as "Manager, Security Engineering" prominently in the hero section
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL present a concise professional summary highlighting his 11+ years of experience in insider threat detection and incident response
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL showcase his advanced training in AI/ML and data-driven leadership within the first viewport
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL display his key achievements including scaling ITD & IR programs across Amazon subsidiaries
5. WHERE a visitor wants quick contact information, THE Portfolio_System SHALL display his email and portfolio URL in an easily accessible format

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a hiring manager in the security field, I want to see Carlos's technical expertise and leadership progression, so that I can evaluate his qualifications for senior security roles.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL organize technical skills into clear categories: Security Engineering, AI/ML & Data Science, and Leadership & Strategy
2. WHEN displaying work experience, THE Portfolio_System SHALL present roles in reverse chronological order with clear progression from Security Engineer to Manager
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL highlight quantifiable achievements such as "processing CloudTrail telemetry from ~4k+ AWS accounts" and "protecting 35.8 million customer accounts"
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL showcase his cross-functional leadership experience across Amazon's hardware subsidiaries (Ring, Blink, Eero, Zoox)
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL display his educational progression including recent executive education from Columbia Business School and Stanford

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a visitor interested in Carlos's work, I want to explore his key projects and achievements in detail, so that I can understand the depth and impact of his contributions.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL create a dedicated projects section showcasing major initiatives like the centralized security analytics platform
2. WHEN a visitor selects a project, THE Portfolio_System SHALL display detailed information including technologies used, challenges solved, and business impact
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL highlight the insider threat program development across Amazon subsidiaries with specific metrics
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL showcase the orchestration middleware security controls project with its impact on 40M+ Ring users
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL present the Two Person Authorization (2PA) implementation with its security benefits

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a mobile user, I want to browse Carlos's portfolio seamlessly on my device, so that I can review his qualifications while on the go.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL provide a fully responsive design that adapts to mobile, tablet, and desktop screen sizes
2. WHEN accessed on mobile devices, THE Portfolio_System SHALL maintain readability and navigation functionality
3. THE Portfolio_System SHALL optimize loading times across all device types
4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL ensure touch-friendly navigation elements on mobile devices
5. THE Portfolio_System SHALL maintain visual hierarchy and content accessibility across all screen sizes

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a potential collaborator, I want to easily contact Carlos and learn about his professional interests, so that I can reach out for relevant opportunities.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Portfolio_System SHALL provide multiple contact methods including email and professional social links
2. THE Portfolio_System SHALL include a contact form for direct inquiries

4. THE Portfolio_System SHALL showcase his interests in AI/ML, security engineering, and data-driven leadership

### Requirement 6
